Hello and welcome. This is an experiment conducted in the context of my (Lefteris Stamellos) master thesis for the Sound and Music Computing master's programme of Universitat Pompeu Fabra, under the title Freesound drumsets using unconventional sounds.

The project's purpose was to to develop an algorithm capable of mapping non-drum sounds to instruments of a standard drum kit. This experiment aims at assessing the resulting system's efficiency.

You will be asked to listen to 20 percussive sequences constructed with random beaten objects (pots, pans, kitchen counters, tubes, etc) or other rapid sounds such as barks, shouts, scratches and hisses. Each sequence is accompanied by three simple questions. We also need you to fill a questionnaire with some personal details so that we can contact you later. Be assured that we take great care in ensuring anonymity of responses! This means that your personal data shall in no case be associated to your questionnaire answers.

You are strongly advised to proceed with the use of headphones. Please try and be honest, there is no right and wrong answer, this is a simple experiment, not a test. It should not take longer than 10 minutes.

One final disclaimer: DO NOT push refresh in any page, otherwise your answers will be lost.
